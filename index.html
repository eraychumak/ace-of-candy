How to play

Setup

Each player starts with 2 cards.

You have a choice between pick-up or stick.

The center of the board is split into two different piles, one being a place to pick up cards and the other for placing them down.

Gameplay

You cannot stick on any number under 16.

If you go over 21 you go bust and lose.

The player that gets 21, or closest to 21 wins the game.

Modes

EASY

Each player has 10 seconds to make a decision. If you go over the time limit, you're out.

NORMAL

Each player has 5 seconds to make a decision. If you go over the time limit, you're out.

HARD

Each player has 2 seconds to make a decision. If you go over the time limit, you're out.
